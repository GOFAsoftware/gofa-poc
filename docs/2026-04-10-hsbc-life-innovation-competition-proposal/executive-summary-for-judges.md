# Executive Summary For HSBC Judges

## Proposal

**HSBC Life ActiveShield**

A prevention-led Health & Wellness Ecosystem that helps HSBC Life evolve from a claims payer into a daily health partner.

## Core Idea

Most insurer relationships are episodic. Customers mainly interact with insurers when they buy a policy, renew, or make a claim. That leaves a major gap between protection and everyday value.

HSBC Life ActiveShield addresses this gap by giving members a simple digital journey that starts with preventive screening, translates risk into clear next steps, and guides them into personalised support such as movement training, healthy-ageing programmes, and ongoing wellness engagement. At the same time, HSBC Life receives aggregate insight into engagement, risk mix, and intervention uptake.

This creates a more relevant customer relationship, supports prevention earlier, and gives HSBC Life a stronger long-term health and wellness proposition.

## Why This Fits HSBC Life's Goal

The competition asks for ideas that reimagine insurance through customer-centricity, health and wellness partnerships, technology adoption, and stronger value propositions. This proposal directly addresses those priorities.

It helps HSBC Life:
- move beyond claims payment into proactive health support
- create visible daily value for members
- combine fitness, healthtech, and healthy-ageing support in one proposition
- use digital insight to improve customer experience and future proposition design

## What The Experience Looks Like

The solution has four connected layers:

1. **Preventive screening**
- short onboarding
- mobility and balance screening
- healthy-ageing indicators

2. **Risk stratification**
- plain-language output
- practical support tiers
- early identification of members who need more support

3. **Personalised intervention**
- AI-guided movement journeys
- healthy-ageing and rehabilitation-style progression
- reminders, engagement loops, and rewards

4. **HSBC Life insight layer**
- assessment coverage
- risk distribution
- engagement and completion metrics
- segment-level reporting for programme refinement

## Business Value For HSBC Life

- stronger differentiation in health and wellness
- more engagement between policy events
- better retention through visible member value
- earlier intervention opportunities for mobility decline and healthy-ageing needs
- stronger foundation for future ecosystem partnerships in movement, nutrition, and digital health

## Why Judges Should Care

This proposal is strong because it is:
- **innovative**: it connects screening, engagement, intervention, and insurer insight in one loop
- **customer-centric**: it produces clear next steps and accessible support instead of generic wellness content
- **commercially credible**: it can be piloted with targeted segments and expanded over time
- **aligned with ESG and sustainability**: it supports preventive care, healthy ageing, and broader access to digital support

## Recommended Partnership Framing

The strongest model is not for HSBC Life to buy a generic vendor tool. It is for HSBC Life and GOFA to co-create a branded wellness proposition in which:
- HSBC Life owns the customer relationship and proposition
- GOFA powers the health engagement, assessment, intervention, and analytics capabilities

## One-Line Pitch

**GOFA helps HSBC Life become a daily health partner by combining insurer-branded engagement, preventive assessment, personalised intervention, and measurable portfolio insight in one ecosystem.**
