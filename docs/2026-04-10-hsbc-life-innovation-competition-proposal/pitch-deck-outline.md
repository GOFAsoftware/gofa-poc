# Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title

**HSBC Life ActiveShield**

**Turning Protection Into Everyday Prevention**

Subtitle:
- A Health & Wellness Ecosystem co-created by HSBC Life and GOFA

Talk track:
- HSBC Life has the opportunity to move from episodic interaction to daily health relevance.
- ActiveShield is a prevention-led ecosystem that combines screening, engagement, intervention, and portfolio insight.

## Slide 2: The Problem

Title:
- Insurance is still too reactive

Key points:
- customers mostly interact at purchase, renewal, or claims
- existing wellness offers are often generic and disconnected
- early movement decline, frailty, and healthy-ageing needs are identified too late
- insurers lack a strong loop between engagement, support, and insight

Talk track:
- The market does not need another static wellness app.
- It needs a model that helps insurers become relevant before risk becomes a claim.

## Slide 3: The Opportunity For HSBC Life

Title:
- Reimagining HSBC Life as a daily health partner

Key points:
- create visible daily value for members
- build stronger customer loyalty and retention
- support healthy ageing and prevention earlier
- open partnership opportunities across fitness, nutrition, and digital health
- generate insight for proposition design and future product strategy

Talk track:
- This is not only a customer-experience opportunity.
- It is also a proposition and commercialisation opportunity.

## Slide 4: The Solution

Title:
- HSBC Life ActiveShield

Visual structure:
- 1. Preventive Screening
- 2. Risk Stratification
- 3. Personalised Intervention
- 4. Insurer Insight Layer

Key points:
- short digital assessment
- plain-language support tiers
- AI-guided movement and healthy-ageing journeys
- engagement loops with rewards and reminders
- insurer-facing dashboards and reporting

Talk track:
- The strength of the solution is that each layer reinforces the next.
- Screening creates insight, insight drives intervention, and engagement generates measurable value.

## Slide 5: Why GOFA Is The Right Partner

Title:
- Built on real GOFA capabilities

Left column: `gofa-web-nextjs`
- insurer-branded engagement layer
- AI Move and wellness journeys
- analytics, rewards, and campaign logic
- operator and portfolio management views

Right column: `sc-gofa-beta1`
- fall-risk and cognitive pathways
- rehab center and lesson progression
- older-adult and healthy-ageing support
- membership and tier packaging

Talk track:
- GOFA is not only offering a concept.
- GOFA already has the core building blocks that make this proposal credible and demoable.

Title:
- Why this should win

Key points by judging lens:

## Slide 7: Pilot Plan And Close

Title:
- Start focused, scale intelligently

Pilot plan:
- Phase 1: launch with healthy-ageing or wellness-focused member segment
- Phase 2: add rewards, ecosystem offers, and richer intervention paths
- Phase 3: expand insurer dashboards and segment-driven proposition refinement

Closing message:
- HSBC Life owns the proposition and customer relationship
- GOFA powers engagement, assessment, intervention, and insight
- together, they can build a new era of prevention-led protection

Final line:
- **HSBC Life ActiveShield helps transform insurance from a promise at claim time into support that customers feel every week.**
